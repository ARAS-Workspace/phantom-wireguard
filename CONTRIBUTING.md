### Contributing to Phantom-WG

This project is managed under a **single-maintainer model**, a decision made during its design phase.

### Why?

`Phantom-WG` is a privacy-focused security tool. Every line of code written directly impacts user privacy and system security. Even in production environments, it operates on a fully open-source architecture; nothing remains hidden.

My core philosophy on this matter:

> `Phantom-WG` must always be functional, reliable for a lifetime, and unwavering in stability.

Adopting the single-maintainer model does not mean I am closed to collaboration. On the contrary, it reflects my deep commitment and sense of responsibility toward this project.

### What I Commit To You

**Continuous Oversight**
I constantly monitor, meticulously review, and improve this codebase. Every change passes through my hands; every line carries my approval.

**Lasting Stability**
Phantom-WG will work tomorrow exactly as it works today. I focus on lasting functionality, not passing trends or popular interfaces.

**Full Transparency**
The code running on your server is identical to the code you see here. No compiled binaries, no hidden modules, no closed doors. From testing processes to the CI/CD pipeline, everything is conducted transparently through this repository.

**Personal Accountability**
When you encounter an issue, you know exactly who to contact. You can be assured that your questions will always receive a response and that I am open to constructive feedback.

### What You Can Do

- **Report Bugs**: Discovered an issue? Open an issue on GitHub. We can discuss it together and find the most suitable solution.
- **Suggest Features**: Have an idea in mind? I would be glad to hear your thoughts.
- **Fork the Project**: Under the AGPL-3.0 license, you are free to create your own version.
- **Study and Learn**: Audit the code, learn from it, use it as a reference in your own projects.
